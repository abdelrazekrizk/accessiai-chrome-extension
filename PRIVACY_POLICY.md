# Privacy Policy for AccessiAI Chrome Extension

**Effective Date**: November 01, 2025  
**Last Updated**: November 01, 2025 

## Overview

AccessiAI is committed to protecting your privacy. This Chrome extension operates with a **privacy-first design** that processes all data locally on your device without sending any personal information to external servers.

## Data Collection and Usage

### What We DON'T Collect
- **No Personal Information**: We do not collect names, emails, or contact details
- **No Browsing History**: We do not track or store your browsing activity
- **No Website Content**: We do not store or transmit webpage content
- **No User Analytics**: We do not send usage data to external servers
- **No Third-Party Tracking**: We do not use analytics services or tracking pixels

### What We DO Store Locally
- **User Preferences**: Settings and customization options (stored locally in your browser)
- **Accessibility Profiles**: Your accessibility preferences and configurations
- **Analysis Results**: Temporary accessibility scan results (automatically cleared)
- **Extension Settings**: Interface preferences and panel positions

## How We Process Data

### Local Processing Only
- **All Analysis**: Accessibility scanning happens entirely on your device
- **No External Servers**: No data is sent to our servers or third parties
- **Encrypted Storage**: Local data is encrypted using AES-GCM encryption
- **Automatic Cleanup**: Temporary data is automatically cleared after use

### Permissions Explained
- **Active Tab**: Required to analyze the current webpage for accessibility issues
- **Storage**: Used to save your preferences and settings locally
- **Text-to-Speech (TTS)**: Optional feature for accessibility enhancements

## Data Security

### Protection Measures
- **Local Encryption**: All stored data is encrypted on your device
- **No Data Transmission**: Zero data leaves your browser
- **Secure Communication**: Internal components use encrypted messaging
- **Regular Security Audits**: Code is regularly reviewed for security vulnerabilities

## Your Rights and Control

### Complete Control
- **Data Ownership**: All data remains on your device under your control
- **Easy Deletion**: Uninstalling the extension removes all associated data
- **Settings Control**: Full control over what data is stored locally
- **Transparency**: Open-source code available for inspection

### How to Delete Your Data
1. **Clear Extension Data**: Go to Chrome Settings > Privacy > Clear browsing data > Extensions
2. **Uninstall Extension**: Remove the extension to delete all associated data
3. **Reset Settings**: Use the extension's reset option to clear preferences

## Third-Party Services

### No Third-Party Data Sharing
- **No Analytics Services**: We do not use Google Analytics or similar services
- **No Advertising Networks**: No ads or tracking pixels
- **No Social Media Integration**: No data sharing with social platforms
- **No Cloud Storage**: All data remains local to your device

## Children's Privacy

AccessiAI does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Since we don't collect personal information from any users, this policy applies to all age groups.

## Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Users will be notified of significant changes through the extension's update mechanism.
